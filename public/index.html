About this app

The idea for this app was born when YouTube recommended me anime opening quiz. I didn't know some of the ones marked as easy (because I didn't watch these animes) but I knew a bunch of openings marked as hard. I came to conclusion that there are no easy and hard ones, there are those you have listened to and the rest. Then I created this app that knows what you have watched and makes a personalized quiz. After half a year, I came across a weeb Discord bot. One of its features was a waifu quiz. No one was able to guess anything because the quiz wasn't personalized. However, a personalized anime character quiz would be a great idea, so I added such a quiz to this app. Now I can't guess the characters I should know.
