App description

The idea for this app was born when Youtube reccomended me anime openings quiz. Actually, I didn't know some of ones marked as easy (because I didn't watch these animes) but I knew a bunch of openings marked as hard. I came to conclusion that there are no easy and hard ones, there are those you have listened to and the rest. Then I created this app that knows what you have watched and makes personelized quiz.

Enjoy!
